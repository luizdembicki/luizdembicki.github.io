Pressure-Temperature Diagram

Pressure-temperature diagrams are very helpful when it comes to determining what is the present phase in a system, specially to chemistry where we tend to work with constant volume.

This diagram was made using two resources: Industrial Formulation 1997 for the Thermodynamic Properties of Water and SteamThe IAPWS , and New Equations for the Sublimation Pressure and Melting Pressure of H2O Ice Ih. The equations programmed in Javascript and plotted using the Plotly library.
